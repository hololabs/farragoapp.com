Markers are pieces of paper that let you puppeteer 3D objects in Farrago. If you assign a marker to a laser-gun, it will look lke you are holding a laser gun in your Farrago movie. You can print out the page above and use these markers with Farrago.

This film was shot with Farrago*, the world’s finest Augmented Reality (AR) Cthulhu-Filmmaking iPhone app.
